capitals at the correct places.

GalaxyNG Engine will use the last one at turn time.  However, do _NOT_
send in orders a few minutes before the turn runs since there is a
chance your orders will be lost.

So for instance to send in orders for turn 105 for the game Zeal use
as subject   "Zeal orders 105"
